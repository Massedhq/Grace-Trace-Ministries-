About Us

A Ministry Built on
Purpose & Compassion

Grace Trace Ministries is a Texas-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to providing safe, structured, and supportive housing for individuals who need it most. We serve returning citizens, homeless veterans, individuals with disabilities including deaf-blind individuals, and others who have been overlooked by our community.

Founded in Frisco, Texas, our programs are built on the belief that every person — regardless of their past — deserves dignity, stability, and the opportunity to thrive. We partner with government agencies, community organizations, and faith-inspired partners to deliver comprehensive housing and support services across Texas.

Our Programs

Housing Solutions for
Every Need

We operate specialized housing programs designed to meet the unique needs of each population we serve.

01 🎖️

Veterans Transitional Housing

Safe, structured housing for veterans experiencing homelessness. We work directly with VA Medical Centers across Texas to provide immediate placement, case management, employment assistance, and a clear path to permanent housing stability.

VA GPD Partner
02 🔓

Reentry Housing Program

Transitional housing for individuals returning from incarceration. Our structured boarding homes provide accountability, drug testing, employment assistance, and life skills training to support successful reintegration into the community.

TDCJ Partner
03 👁️

DBMD Residential Care

Specialized residential care for deaf-blind individuals with multiple disabilities under the Texas Medicaid DBMD waiver. Our trained staff provide Intervener services, tactile communication support, and individualized care plans.

Medicaid DBMD
04 🏘️

Young Adults & Transitioning Youth

For young adults and individuals with developmental or intellectual disabilities, the term often refers to the life skills required to transition out of the family home or the foster care system. It involves training in personal finance, meal preparation, employment, and housing, allowing individuals to successfully manage their day-to-day lives without adult supervision.

HHSC Licensed

Our Impact

Building a Foundation
One Life at a Time

Government-Backed Programs That Deliver Real Results

Grace Trace Ministries partners with the Texas Department of Criminal Justice, the Bureau of Prisons, the Department of Veterans Affairs, and Texas HHSC to deliver evidence-based housing programs that change lives.

Our structured approach combines safe housing with life skills training, employment assistance, and ongoing case management — giving every resident the tools they need to succeed long-term.
